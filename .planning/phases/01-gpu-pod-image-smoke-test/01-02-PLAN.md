| T-01-02-02 | Tampering (supply chain) | sudoingX community gist | medium | mitigate | Content is committed VERBATIM into the repo (not fetched at runtime). | T-01-02-04 | Information Disclosure | Template content | low | accept | Template is public (gist is public). No secrets embedded. |
